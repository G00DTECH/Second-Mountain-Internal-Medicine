The Problem

For some seniors, getting to the doctor can be tough. Many families are left scrambling to coordinate transportation, manage multiple specialists, and worry about whether their loved one is getting the care they need.

The Solution

We bring the doctor to them. Dr. Matulis provides primary care in the comfort of your loved one's home—managing chronic conditions, coordinating with specialists, and keeping patients out of the hospital.

Common Questions

Is this real primary care, or just one-time visits?

This is real, ongoing primary care. Dr. Matulis becomes your primary care physician—managing your conditions over time, not just checking in once.

What if my mom needs to go to the ER?

Call 911 for emergencies. We'll follow up after any ER visit or hospitalization to make sure the transition home goes smoothly.

Does this cost extra?

For most Traditional Medicare patients, there's no additional cost beyond standard Medicare copays. We don't charge concierge fees.
