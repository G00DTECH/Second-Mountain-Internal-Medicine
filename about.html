About Second Mountain

A home-based primary care practice rooted in the Oxford Hills

Why "Second Mountain"?

The name "Second Mountain" comes from a concept popularized by author David Brooks: the idea that after climbing the first mountain of career success and personal achievement, many people discover a second mountain—one focused on service, commitment, and deeper purpose.

For Dr. Matulis, home-based primary care is that second mountain. It's medicine the way it used to be practiced—and the way it should be for patients who can't easily leave home.

For the patients we serve, this is often their second mountain too: a stage of life focused on what matters most—health, dignity, and time with family.

What Longitudinal Care Means

Longitudinal care means we're not here for a single visit. Dr. Matulis becomes your ongoing primary care physician—the doctor who knows your history, understands your goals, and sees you regularly over months and years.

This isn't urgent care at home. It's a relationship.

About Dr. Matulis

After years caring for older adults in traditional internal medicine settings, Dr. Matulis recognized that many patients need something different — more time, deeper relationships, and a physician who truly knows them — which led to the creation of Second Mountain Internal Medicine, P.C.
