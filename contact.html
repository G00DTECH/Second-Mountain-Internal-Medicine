How to Get Started

For Families

Getting started is simple:

  1. Call or text us at 207-492-4642. Tell us about your loved one and their situation.
  2. We verify Medicare eligibility. We'll confirm that your loved one has Traditional Medicare and meets the criteria for home-based care.
  3. We schedule the first home visit. Dr. Matulis will come to the home, meet the patient and family, review medical history, and create a care plan.
  4. Ongoing care begins. Regular visits, available by phone, and coordination with other providers.
