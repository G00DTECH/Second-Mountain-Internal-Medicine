We welcome partnerships from foundations, corporations, volunteers, and service providers

For Grant Makers & Foundations

Maine Winter Survival Initiative offers foundations a unique opportunity to fund the only systematic survival equipment distribution program in Maine—filling a documented gap with measurable, life-saving impact.

Year 1 Funding Need: $157,000

Serving 500 individuals in Portland with 1,000 survival kits and 6,000 MREs

Why Fund MWSI?

✓
Documented Gap: No existing program systematically distributes survival equipment and MREs to Maine's 281 unsheltered individuals
✓
Immediate Impact: Each kit provides protection to -30°F plus 3 days nutrition—directly preventing exposure deaths
✓
Veteran-Founded: Qualifies for veteran-focused foundation grants with authentic lived experience
✓
Partnership Model: Extends capacity of established organizations rather than competing for resources
✓
Housing Pathways: Every distribution connects individuals to Coordinated Entry and permanent housing programs
✓
Scalable Model: Clear 3-year expansion plan from Portland to Lewiston to Bangor

For Volunteers

Help us distribute life-saving equipment and connect unsheltered individuals to housing resources.

Volunteer Opportunities

Street Outreach Teams

Join our outreach teams distributing kits directly to unsheltered individuals. Training provided on trauma-informed approaches and housing navigation.

Kit Assembly

Help assemble survival kits at our warehouse, ensuring each contains all critical components for winter survival.

Distribution Logistics

Support transportation and delivery of kits to partner organizations and outreach locations across the service area.

Skills-Based Volunteering

Contribute professional skills in grant writing, marketing, web development, or financial management.

Time Commitment

Flexible opportunities from one-time events to regular weekly shifts. All volunteers receive training and ongoing support.

For Corporate Partners

Partner with MWSI to demonstrate your company's commitment to community impact while engaging employees in meaningful service.

Corporate Partnership Opportunities

Corporate Sponsorship

Fund specific programs with recognition in our materials, reports, and website. Year 1 sponsorship levels: $5,000 | $10,000 | $25,000 | $50,000+

Employee Engagement

Organize employee volunteer days for kit assembly, distribution events, or fundraising campaigns. Strengthen team culture while making community impact.

In-Kind Donations

Donate sleeping bags, blankets, MREs, hand warmers, or other survival equipment. We can coordinate bulk purchases at nonprofit pricing.

Matching Gift Programs

Amplify employee giving through corporate matching programs, doubling the impact of individual contributions to MWSI.

Benefits of Partnership

  • ✓ Demonstrate corporate social responsibility with measurable community impact
  • ✓ Engage employees in meaningful volunteer experiences
  • ✓ Recognition in annual reports, website, and marketing materials
  • ✓ Impact reports showing lives saved through your partnership

For Service Providers

We partner with shelters, outreach organizations, and service providers to extend your capacity and better serve unsheltered individuals.

Partnership Opportunities

Distribution Partners

Request survival kits for your clients. We'll stock your organization with kits for direct distribution to those you serve.

Joint Outreach

Coordinate street outreach efforts, combining our survival equipment with your case management and housing navigation services.

Referral Network

Become part of our housing connection network, receiving direct referrals from our distribution teams to your programs.

Data Sharing

Share outcome data to strengthen our collective understanding of what works in preventing exposure deaths and connecting people to housing.

Current Partners

We're building relationships with Preble Street, Portland Housing Authority, Trinity Jubilee Center, Bangor Area Homeless Shelter, and other established organizations across Maine.
