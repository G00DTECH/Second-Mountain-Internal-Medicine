Our Programs

Life-saving support delivered with dignity and compassion

Meeting Critical Needs

Our programs address the immediate survival needs of Maine's unsheltered population while creating pathways to permanent housing solutions.

Winter Survival Kits

Comprehensive survival equipment designed to protect against Maine's deadly winter conditions.

Each Kit Contains:

  • ✓ 0°F Sleeping Bag - Military-grade cold weather protection
  • ✓ Emergency Blankets - Thermal reflective material
  • ✓ Hand & Foot Warmers - Up to 8 hours of heat
  • ✓ Weatherproof Tarp - Wind and rain protection
  • ✓ Wool Blanket - Additional insulation layer

Protection Rating: Survival to -30°F wind chill conditions

MRE Nutrition Program

Shelf-stable, high-calorie meals providing critical nutrition without requiring cooking facilities.

Program Features:

  • ✓ No Preparation Required - Ready-to-eat meals
  • ✓ 3 Days Nutrition - Minimum 6 MREs per distribution
  • ✓ 1,200+ Calories Each - High energy content
  • ✓ 12-Month Shelf Life - Can be stored safely
  • ✓ Variety of Options - Multiple meal choices

Distribution: 6,000+ MREs in Year 1, scaling to 27,000 by Year 3

Street Outreach Program

Meeting people where they are with dignity, respect, and immediate support.

Our Approach:

  • ✓ Regular Routes - Consistent presence in known locations
  • ✓ Emergency Response - Rapid deployment in severe weather
  • ✓ No Barriers - No ID, sobriety, or service requirements
  • ✓ Respectful Interactions - Dignity-first approach
  • ✓ Partnership Coordination - Work with existing services

Service Areas: Portland, Lewiston, and Bangor by Year 3

Housing Connections

Every interaction is an opportunity to connect individuals with pathways to permanent housing.

Connection Services:

  • ✓ Coordinated Entry - Direct connection to Maine's housing system
  • ✓ Resource Cards - Contact info for all regional services
  • ✓ MaineHousing Programs - Information on available assistance
  • ✓ Rapid Rehousing - Connections to placement services
  • ✓ Partner Referrals - Direct links to service providers

Goal: Survival equipment sustains life, housing restores it
